Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

LeadingLeads.co ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a life insurance quote, we collect:

  • Your name, date of birth, gender, state, and ZIP code
  • Health information you provide (height, weight, smoking status, self-rated health, listed conditions)
  • Your phone number (required) and email address (optional)
  • The policy type and coverage amount you request

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate a personalized insurance quote estimate
  • To send a one-time SMS verification code to your phone
  • To follow up with you about your quote, including by phone, SMS, or email
  • To match you with licensed insurance agents who may contact you with offers

3. SMS Messaging Consent

By submitting your phone number and checking the consent box on our quote form, you expressly consent to receive SMS messages from LeadingLeads.co, including:

  • A one-time verification code when you submit a quote request
  • Follow-up messages from licensed agents regarding your quote

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with our service. You may receive up to 5 messages per quote request.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. LeadingLeads.co does not charge for these messages, but your carrier may.

Opting Out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message we send you. After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further SMS messages from us. To re-subscribe, reply START.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any SMS from us, or email support@leadingleads.co.

Carrier Disclaimer

Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Carriers supported include AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, U.S. Cellular, and others.

4. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Licensed insurance agents and carriers who can provide you with policy offers
  • Service providers who help us operate our service (e.g. Twilio for SMS delivery)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your information to unrelated third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS) and limited employee access. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing support@leadingleads.co. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what information we have collected about them.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at support@leadingleads.co.
