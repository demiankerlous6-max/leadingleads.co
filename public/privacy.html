Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

LeadingLeads.co ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and how we use it. LeadingLeads.co is a lead-generation service that connects prospective insurance customers with licensed insurance agents. We are not a licensed insurance broker or carrier.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a final expense insurance estimate, we collect:

  • Your name, date of birth (or age), gender, and state
  • Limited health information you choose to provide (such as nicotine use)
  • The coverage type and coverage amount you request
  • Your phone number (required) and email address (optional)
  • Your express consent and timestamp when you opt in to be contacted

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate a personalized insurance estimate based on the information you provide
  • To send a one-time SMS verification code to confirm ownership of the phone number you supplied
  • If, and only if, you expressly opt in by checking the consent box and verifying your phone number, to share your contact information with a licensed insurance agent so they can follow up with you about your estimate
  • To improve and operate our website

3. SMS Messaging Consent

Submitting our estimate form does not trigger any SMS message. An SMS verification code is only sent if you affirmatively check the consent box on the results page. By checking that box, you expressly consent to receive (1) a one-time 6-digit SMS verification code from LeadingLeads.co at the phone number you provided, and (2) a follow-up phone call from a licensed insurance agent referred by LeadingLeads.co. This consent is not a condition of any purchase, and you can withdraw it at any time as described below.

Message Frequency

Under normal use you will receive one verification code per estimate session. You may receive an additional code if you request a re-send.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. LeadingLeads.co does not charge for these messages, but your carrier may.

Opting Out

You can opt out of further SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You can withdraw your consent to be contacted by phone at any time by emailing the address listed below.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any SMS from us, or email demiankerlous6@gmail.com.

Carrier Disclaimer

Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

4. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Licensed insurance agents — only after you expressly opt in by checking the consent box on the results page and verifying your phone number
  • Service providers who help us operate the service (for example, Vonage for SMS verification delivery and Google for cloud-based data storage)
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your information to third-party marketers.

5. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS). No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing demiankerlous6@gmail.com. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what information we have collected about them and the right to request deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made.
