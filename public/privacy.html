This policy explains what personal information we collect through this website, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using this site or submitting the quote form, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

When you request an estimate, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number;
  • Information you enter to estimate a quote (for example, age, gender, state, coverage type and amount, nicotine use);
  • The fact and timestamp of your consent, the version of the consent text you saw, and the page you submitted from.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate an estimated insurance quote for you;
  • To send a one-time SMS verification code that confirms ownership of the phone number you supplied;
  • If, and only if, you expressly opt in by checking the consent box and verifying your phone number, to share your contact information with one or more licensed insurance agents and their agencies ("marketing partners") so they can follow up with you about your estimate;
  • To operate, secure, and improve the site, and to comply with law.

3. How we share or sell your information

We share the information you submit with one or more licensed insurance agents and their agencies ("marketing partners") so they can contact you about coverage. Under New Jersey law, providing your information to those agents may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal data. Aside from those marketing partners, we share your information only with providers that help us run the site (for example, Vonage for SMS verification delivery and Google for cloud-based data storage) or as required by law. You can request a current list of the marketing partners we share with by contacting us at the email below — see also our Who May Contact You page.

We do not sell sensitive personal data, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

4. Your privacy rights (New Jersey residents)

Subject to the New Jersey Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Confirm whether we are processing your personal data and access it;
  • Correct inaccuracies in your personal data;
  • Delete your personal data;
  • Obtain a portable copy of your data;
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal data, of targeted advertising, and of certain profiling.

5. How to exercise your rights / opt out

You can opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information at any time using the Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information link in our website footer, or by emailing us at Mhannalla@outlook.com. We also honor recognized universal opt-out signals (such as Global Privacy Control) sent by your browser — if your browser sends this signal, we will automatically register an opt-out for your phone number when you submit a form. We will respond to verified requests within the timeframe required by law.

6. SMS messaging consent

Submitting our estimate form does not trigger any SMS message. An SMS verification code is only sent if you affirmatively check the consent box on the results page. By checking that box, you expressly consent to receive (1) a one-time 6-digit SMS verification code from Leading Leads Co. at the phone number you provided, and (2) a follow-up phone call from a licensed insurance agent referred by Leading Leads Co. This consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message and data rates may apply; message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out of texts at any time.

7. Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
